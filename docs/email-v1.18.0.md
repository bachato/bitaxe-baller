Hey there,

Bitaxe Baller **v1.18.0** just dropped, and it's a big one. Here's what's new.

## 🔧 Update your whole fleet's firmware — in one click

Last release, Baller started *telling* you when your miners were running old AxeOS firmware. Now it *does the update for you.*

- **Pro:** hit **Update all →** on the notice bar. Baller grabs the vetted files and flashes every selected miner — one at a time, in the right order, with live per-device progress. It stops if one fails, so a bad release can't take out your whole fleet.
- **Free:** update a single miner from its device page — Baller handles the two-file flash and verifies it for you.

And it's **fail-closed and Bitaxe-only** by design: it will never flash a NerdQAxe, or a Bitaxe running custom firmware, so it can't push the wrong image onto your board.

## 🎲 See your real block odds

Every per-coin fleet tile now shows your actual solo-mining odds from your combined hashrate — *"1 in 5,000 this year"* on Bitcoin, or *"~7 blocks/year"* on easily-mined chains like eCash where a fleet realistically hits blocks. The lottery, finally quantified.

## 🖥️ Now monitors NerdQAxe

Running a NerdQAxe (or another AxeOS-based miner) next to your Bitaxes? Baller now watches them all on one screen — hashrate, temps, chain detection, and block odds, with multi-ASIC boards measured correctly.

## 🛠️ Smoother and more stable

We squashed two rare dashboard freezes where a slow network call could briefly hang the poll loop. Recommended update for everyone.

**How to update:** the Mac and Windows apps update themselves — just relaunch. On Umbrel, it's in your app store.
